About

Where Candy Meets Creativity

Cricket's Candy Creations' mission is to provide innovative educational experiences and a unique outlet for exploration, visualization, creativity, and fun.

Our Staten Island location is locally owned and operated, bringing the same award-winning experience that made the NYC location a media sensation (TODAY Show, Blippi, Meekah!) right here to our community.

"I wanted to provide a space for kids — and the young at heart — to let loose, explore, engage, create and of course eat!"
